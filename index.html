Store Optimization

FROM FRICTION TO
CLEARER SHOPPING.

❌  BEFORE — COMMON FRICTION POINTS
❌
Unclear value proposition — visitors can't tell what makes the product different
❌
Weak CTA hierarchy — Add to Cart is hard to find on mobile
❌
Missing trust signals — no reviews near the buy button
❌
Poor mobile spacing — elements crowd on smaller screens
❌
Confusing product options — variant selectors are unclear
✅  AFTER — OPTIMIZED FOR CLARITY
✅
Clearer value proposition — headline communicates the benefit immediately
✅
Stronger primary CTA — high-contrast button easy to tap on any device
✅
Visible trust signals — reviews and guarantees near the purchase decision
✅
Improved mobile spacing — generous padding and structured sections
✅
Simplified product options — clean, intuitive selectors with clear labels

Example improvements shown for demonstration. Final recommendations depend on the store, customers, products, and available data.
