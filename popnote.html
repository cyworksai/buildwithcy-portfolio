Project Overview

A product page that does more than display an item.

PopNote AI is a Shopify portfolio project for a modern AI note-taking product. I created it to show how a single-product store can explain a tech product clearly while still looking polished, premium, and easy to shop.

The goal was to create a product page experience that guides customers through what the product does, why it is useful, what features it offers, and why it is worth considering — without feeling technical or intimidating.

The Challenge

AI products are easy to over-explain

Tech products often lose customers by going too deep too soon. The challenge was to present an AI product in a way that feels immediately useful and human — not like a features list from a developer's README.

The Approach

Clarity before complexity

I structured the page to lead with the transformation — what the customer's life looks like with the product — before introducing features. Benefits first, mechanics second.

Visual Design & Product Assets

Modern, tech-inspired, and built for product clarity.

I created and prepared the visual assets used throughout the page — product mockups, website banners, feature graphics, badges, and promotional-style visuals — using Canva and Photoshop.

The visual direction is sleek, modern, and product-focused: strong contrast, clean spacing, and polished visuals that make the product feel premium without making it feel unapproachable.

Design Direction

Premium without complexity

I used strong contrast, clean spacing, and simple messaging so visitors can quickly understand the product's value without being overwhelmed by technical language or dense layouts.

Asset Creation

Mockups and page visuals

Product mockups, feature graphics, and promotional banners were created to make the page feel more realistic and polished — as if it were a real product ready to sell.

Why visual assets matter for tech products

AI products are abstract by nature — customers can't touch or hold them. Polished visuals do the work of making the product feel real, valuable, and worth the price before the customer has read a single word of copy.
