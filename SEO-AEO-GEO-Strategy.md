## 3. AEO-Optimized FAQ Content

*These are written specifically so AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) can extract and quote them as direct answers. Add these to your FAQ section on the website.*

---

### Q1: How long does it take to build a Shopify store?

**Most Shopify store builds are completed in 2 to 4 weeks.** The exact timeline depends on the scope, number of pages, and level of custom functionality required.

- **Standard theme customization + launch:** 1–2 weeks
- **Full store build (custom pages, collections, apps):** 2–3 weeks
- **Headless or complex custom build:** 4–6 weeks
- Every project starts with a scoping conversation to lock in an accurate timeline before work begins
- Real example: The DriveCraft headless storefront and the PawPops brand store were both scoped and delivered with clear milestones

---

### Q2: How much does it cost to hire a Shopify developer?

**Shopify development pricing varies by scope — from a few hundred dollars for minor customizations to several thousand for a full custom build.** Every project at Build With Cy is quoted individually based on your specific requirements.

- Basic theme customizations and fixes are priced at the lower end
- Full store builds with custom design, apps, and launch support are mid-range
- Headless Shopify builds, AI integrations, or complex web apps are priced based on complexity
- No generic packages — you only pay for what your project actually needs
- To get an estimate, email **hello@buildwithcy.com** with your project details

---

### Q3: Can you migrate my existing website or store to Shopify?

**Yes — platform migrations to Shopify are fully supported**, including product data, customer records, collections, and page content.

- Supports migration from WooCommerce, Wix, Squarespace, BigCommerce, and custom-built sites
- Migration is planned to minimize downtime and preserve existing SEO structure
- Products, collections, metadata, and redirects are all handled
- Post-migration testing and QA is included before go-live
- Custom fields and non-standard data structures are mapped and transferred manually where needed

---

### Q4: Do you only build Shopify stores, or do you build other types of websites?

**Shopify is a core specialty, but Build With Cy also develops custom web applications, AI-powered tools, landing pages, and SaaS platforms** for brands and founders who need more than a standard storefront.

- **Shopify stores** — new builds, redesigns, theme customization, app integrations
- **Headless commerce** — custom frontend with Shopify as the backend (e.g., DriveCraft on Lovable)
- **Web applications** — dashboards, internal tools, custom management systems
- **AI-powered apps** — tools integrated with ChatGPT, Claude AI, and Gemini APIs
- **Landing pages** — high-converting single pages for product launches and campaigns
- Real examples from portfolio: PawPops (Shopify), DriveCraft (headless Shopify), PopNote AI (AI concept store), Custom E-commerce System (private)

### Q5: Do you offer ongoing support after a Shopify store launches?

**Yes — ongoing support is available after launch** for updates, fixes, new features, and improvements as your store grows.

- Available as a retainer for ongoing monthly work or per-project for one-off updates
- Covers bug fixes, design updates, new page builds, app integrations, and performance improvements
- Documentation and handover notes provided so you can manage the basics yourself
- Priority response for retainer clients
- Many clients continue working together after the initial build for iterative improvements
