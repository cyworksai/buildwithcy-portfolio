Project Overview

More than a storefront — a full operations system.

This project was built as an internal e-commerce operations system for a real product-based business. The business needed more than a normal storefront — its workflow involved manual payments, uploaded receipts, affiliate codes, partner groups, first-time vouchers, repeat customers, manual orders, order status updates, commissions, and role-based access.

I created a custom system that supports the way the business actually operates. Customers can place orders, affiliates can track earnings and referrals, partners can monitor team performance, employees can help process manual orders, and admins can manage the full workflow from one dashboard.

The Challenge

Off-the-shelf tools weren't enough

The business had workflows that standard Shopify or simple SaaS tools couldn't handle — manual payments, affiliate crediting, partner group ownership, employee-assisted ordering, and admin-level commission corrections all needed to work together.

The Solution

A custom system for real operations

I planned and built a full-stack web application with separate portals for customers, affiliates, partners, employees, and admins — each with only the access and actions relevant to their role.

Manual Orders & Order Tracking

Handling orders the way the business actually works.

Since the business handles manual payments, uploaded receipts, affiliate-assisted purchases, chat orders, phone orders, and repeat customers, the system needed a clear way to create, review, update, and track orders outside of a standard checkout.

Manual Orders

Employee-Created Orders

Employees can create orders manually for customers who order through chat, phone, Viber, repeat purchase messages, affiliate assistance, or other manual sales channels.

Order Tracking

Status-Based Workflow

Orders move through clear statuses — pending, paid, processing, shipped, completed, cancelled — so the team always knows where an order stands.

  • Manual order creation from employee or admin side
  • Support for chat, phone, Viber, and assisted customer orders
  • Uploaded receipt review workflow and payment verification
  • Order status tracking from pending through shipped, delivered, and cancelled
  • Referrer and affiliate connection to orders
  • Customer phone matching for repeat orders
  • Discount and voucher handling
  • Commission connection for affiliate-assisted orders
  • Export and missing commission repair tools
  • Activity logs for order updates and admin actions
Affiliate, Referral & Commission Logic

Credit the right person every time.

The business needed a way to track which affiliate or partner group should receive credit when customers use referral links, discount codes, or order manually. I designed the logic around affiliate codes, referral links, recruitment links, repeat purchases, partner groups, commission rates, payout status, and team bonus tracking.

Commissions

Auto & manual commission records

Auto commissions triggered from referral links, manual commissions assigned by admin, confirmed payout tracking, released commission records, and commission filters by status and source.

Referral Tools

Links, codes & recruitment

Each affiliate has a referral link, discount code, and recruitment link. Copy buttons, commission rate display, team bonus visibility, and marketing materials are all accessible from the affiliate dashboard.
